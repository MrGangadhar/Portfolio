# ADSR Analytics Audit Case Study

## Client Context
ADSR needed campaign performance visibility across multiple ad accounts where raw data was fragmented in CSV and Excel exports.

## Problem
- Inconsistent naming conventions across campaigns and ad groups
- Missing UTM tags in multiple conversion paths
- Duplicate rows and timezone mismatches in exported reports
- No standardized executive summary for weekly decision-making

## Objective
Build a reliable audit and reporting flow to transform raw data into trustworthy visual insights and actionable recommendations.

## Approach
1. Collected raw files from ad platforms and CRM exports
2. Cleaned and standardized dimensions:
   - campaign_name
   - source_medium
   - date (timezone-normalized)
3. Validated KPIs:
   - impressions, clicks, spend
   - CTR, CPC, CPA, ROAS
4. Performed anomaly checks:
   - sudden spend spikes
   - conversion drop patterns
   - broken attribution windows
5. Prepared client-facing outputs:
   - KPI dashboard
   - audit findings report
   - executive summary deck

## Audit Framework Used
- Data completeness score
- Tracking hygiene score
- Metric integrity score
- Insight readiness score

## Outcome Snapshot
- Reduced reporting preparation time by approximately 55%
- Improved confidence in weekly KPI reviews
- Identified top 5 leakage areas for optimization
- Delivered 30/60/90 day action roadmap

## Deliverables
- Dashboard (weekly refresh)
- Audit report with issue severity and owner mapping
- Executive summary for leadership stakeholders

## Tools
Excel, SQL, Power BI, Google Sheets, documented QA checklist
